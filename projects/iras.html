ReTeam is a MS Teams like iOS application designed for professional teams, businesses, HR, coaches and mentors in order to enhance digital interaction with co-workers, foster trust, respect, and increase team productivity. Whenever we need remote teams who are not co-located, ReTeam can provide support.

- Developed new UI designs for Bright ideas and posts.

- Continuously Worked on bug fixing and improving application performance.
